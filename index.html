The
Little

Mermaid

Once upon a time, there was a little mermaid princess who lived in an underwater kingdom. She had the most enchanting voice in the entire world.
After years of dreaming to catch a glimpse of the world above, she courageously swam up to the surface. She saw breathtaking fireworks. It was a lavish birthday celebration for a handsome prince. To her surprise, she immediately fell in love with him.
Longing for the Prince and an immortal soul, she visited the cave of a Sea Witch in the most dangerous part of the ocean. She made a daring deal with the Witch. In exchange for a magic potion that would transform her tail into a pair of legs, the Little Mermaid sold her beautiful voice to the Witch and she became mute.
The Sea Witch warned her becoming human would come at the cost of forever suffering from the pain of losing her tail, and once human, she would never be able to return to the sea.
But little did she know that the Prince was in love with another. The Little Mermaid was heartbroken.
She cried an ocean of tears until she dissolved into sea foam upon the waves. The Little Mermaid had returned to her home - the sea.
